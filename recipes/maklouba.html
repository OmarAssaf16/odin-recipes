Maklouba Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken or Lamb
  • Eggplant
  • Tomatoes
  • Spices (cumin, turmeric, etc.)
  • Garnish (nuts, parsley)

Steps

  1. Prepare the meat and vegetables
  2. Cook the rice with spices
  3. Layer the ingredients in a pot
  4. Cook until everything is tender
  5. Invert onto a serving platter
  6. Garnish and serve
